        About Our Expense Tracker


        Welcome to Financely, your reliable companion for managing and tracking your personal finances efficiently. Our platform empowers users to take control of their spending habits, allowing for better financial planning and organization through an easy-to-use interface.

        Why Financely?


        In today's fast-paced world, keeping track of daily expenses can be challenging. Whether you're managing a household budget or tracking personal finances, Financely simplifies the process by offering a user-friendly way to log, categorize, and analyze your financial transactions.

        Key Features:


        User Registration and Secure Login: Create an account to safely store your transactions and track your expenses over time. Add, View, and Manage Transactions: Easily add new transactions, including the date, amount, category, and description. View your transaction history in one place.

        Categorized Transactions: Organize expenses into categories like food, travel, utilities, and more for better insight into where your money is going.

        Date Range and Category Filtering: Find transactions based on custom date ranges or specific categories.

        View Transaction Summaries: Get summaries of the last few transactions or based on specific categories, making it easier to monitor spending trends.


        Technology Stack:



        Frontend: The intuitive and dynamic user interface is built using modern web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, delivering a seamless experience across devices.

        Backend: Our backend system is powered by C++, offering optimized performance for managing user authentication and transaction data. It's connected to the frontend using Express.js, a fast and flexible web framework for handling API requests.

        Database: User and transaction data is securely stored and managed using file-based storage in the backend, with plans to integrate more scalable solutions in future updates.

        Authentication: We ensure user security with a login system that protects your credentials and data, offering a smooth and secure experience.


        At Financely, we believe in simplicity and efficiency, helping you keep your finances organized without the hassle. Whether you're saving for a big goal or just trying to stay on top of daily spending, our platform is here to support you.

        Start managing your expenses with Financely today, and take the first step toward financial freedom!
